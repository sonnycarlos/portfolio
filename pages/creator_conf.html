Creator Conf

The world's largest conference for creators. Join the world's top creators to learn, share secrets and take their audiences to the next.

Year

2025

Role

Web Designer

Creator Conf is a premier virtual conference designed for content creators aiming to enhance their skills, grow their audiences, and monetize their platforms. The event brings together over 5,000 creators for two days of intensive learning and networking.

I was assigned to redesign the website for the upcoming 2025 conference.

Hero Section

The previous design of Creator Conf used a light theme across all sections, which, while clean, lacked the premium feel and savvy energy expected from a conference of this scale. To enhance the brand and provide a more engaging, upscale experience, I transitioned to a cinematic dark theme that adds depth, contrast, and a sense of exclusivity.

Past Speakers Section

With a large number of speakers at past events, displaying all of them at once would have made the website feel overwhelming. To ensure a more organized and visually engaging experience, I made it an animated carousel. This will make easier for visitors to explore past speakers in a more intuitive way.
