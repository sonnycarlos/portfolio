AWC Networking App

Your all-in-one networking platform for Affiliate World events—match with peers, chat, meet, and manage leads.

Year

2025

Role

UI/UX Designer

Graphic Designer

Affiliate World’s Networking App is your all-access pass to smarter, faster networking. Use it to browse attendees, match with ideal partners, and secure meetings before the event even begins. Scan badges with one tap, rate your leads, and manage your follow-ups without spreadsheets. Plus, you get full access to the event agenda, speaker bios, and venue details—right from your pocket. It’s everything you need to turn handshakes into deals.

Featuring the Affiliate World Europe Edition.
