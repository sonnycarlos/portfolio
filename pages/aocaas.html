Academy of Creator Arts and Sciences (AOCAAS) is the premier gala evening celebrating the best influencers and creators of the world!

Logo

My approach to designing the AOCAAS logo was based in the idea of prestige, recognition, and creative brilliance. As a reference, I utilized a man trophy, which stands for honor and accomplishment. The angular elements in the logo convey strength and forward motion, capturing the inventiveness spirit of creators.

This final design embodies AOCAAS's mission of celebrating creative excellence on a global stage.
