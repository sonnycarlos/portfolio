Tribbo is an Edtech Startup for people which supports their professional careers with the help of communities and mentors using modern education methodologies. They help people to find purpose, gain skills, and connect with top companies all over the world. I was responsible for product design, user interface and user experience design, WordPress development, and specially the branding.

Enhanced Brand Perception

Tribbo aims to revamp its website, Through the development of the revamped website featuring improved user experience and a new visual identity, we wanted to give Tribbo a platform to showcase its mission and goal, and to foster meaningful B2B and B2C connections. And also, our goal is to double the leads generated by having a revamped website.

Programs Stacks

We conducted an A/B testing for the website, focusing particularly on program offerings. User interface and user experience design are very crucial for a website. We showed them different layouts and surveyed them which layout they preferred.

We have to stand out over the other competitors and attract potential clients to purchase program offerings by employing design strategies with the help of A/B testing.

Conclusion

I am happy to be a part of Tribbo, where I helped increase the sales of their programs through web design. I wanted to make the design more modern by adding cool animations, but the client wanted to keep the design simple and plain. The strategic design choices we made not only improved customer engagement but also optimized the conversion process, leading to a noticeable increase in sales for the company.
