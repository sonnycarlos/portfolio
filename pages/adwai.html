Hero Section's Content Alignment Rule

Alignment of Content

Make sure that the content of the hero section is vertically centered between the bottom of the navigation bar and the bottom of the viewport height.

The goal is to ensure that the contents are visually balanced when viewed, perfectly centered within the available screen space below the navigation.

Adjusted Alignment of Content with Price Increase Bar

Make sure the content of the hero section is vertically centered between the bottom of the navigation bar and the top of the price increase bar.

If the price increase bar is visible, the hero content should adjust its center point between these two sections to keep the layout visually balanced.
