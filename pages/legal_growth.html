Role

Web Designer

Legal Growth is an Atlanta-based web design and search engine optimization (SEO) agency focused on providing organic local SEO and digital marketing services. Their goal is to support companies in achieving success with their projects on the web, striving to make digital strategies more efficient, and to put the right tools on the side for web communication at the service of the development of sales and notoriety.

Strategy

To gain an advantage over competitors, I thought of using a magazine theme for our website design since our clients are law firms and lawyers. This strategy makes it easier for visitors to understand the services and the type of clients the company caters to, and by presenting information in an engaging and visually appealing format, we can effectively showcase the company’s expertise and attract more potential clients.

Splash Screen

I added cool animations to the design to make the company stand out. I noticed that their competitors only have plain designs which are boring and not attractive. This strategy will show potential clients that the company is updated on design trends.

Note: I used only pure CSS for the animations here and no libraries whatsoever.
